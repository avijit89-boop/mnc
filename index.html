ABOUT

SRI KRISHNA MESS

Welcome to SRI KRISHNA MESS, your home away from home! Nestled in the heart of BARASAT, Kajibari, we offer a comfortable and affordable living experience for students and young professionals. Our mission is to provide a welcoming and secure environment where you can thrive academically and socially.

At SRI KRISHNA MESS, we understand the importance of a balanced lifestyle. Our facilities include spacious rooms, high-speed internet, and communal areas designed to foster connections among residents.

Celebrate Your Birthday with Us! At SRI KRISHNA MESS, we believe every birthday is a special occasion. Join us for a memorable experience filled with joy, laughter, and delicious food.
